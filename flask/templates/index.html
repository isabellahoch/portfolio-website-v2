She is fluent in French, speaks conversational Spanish and German, has beginning proficiency in Latin and Italian, and has earned several academic awards including the President’s Award for Academic Achievement, NCWIT Aspirations Honorable Mention in 2018 and 2019, and the 2018 Congressional App Challenge for District CA-15. portfolio

Fight Fast Fashion

JULY 2020 - PRESENT

When users visit fightfastfashion.com, they can search for sustainable products based on keywords or by inputting a link to a fast fashion item. They are then redirected to a search page that shows a loading screen before revealing sustainable products with links to the retailers that sell them. In the footer, there are also direct links to shop at some featured retailers and resources to get educated about the issue. For this project, I used a Python Flask backend with HTML/CSS/JS templates and a Firebase database.

BUDDI

JANUARY 2020 - APRIL 2020

In light of the current global pandemic, we wanted to create an app that would help one of the highest at-risk populations for COVID-19. During the 2020 PixelHacks IV Hackathon, we developed BUDDI, a companion tool that easily provides assistance and resources to senior citizens with a friendly chatbot and many other interactive features! This app ended up winning Best Use of Google Cloud and Domain at the hackathon, and was subsequently entered in the COVIDATHON: Decentralized AI Hackathon, where it placed second overall and won a 60,000 OCEAN token prize (roughly $10,000 USD). Responsibilities for this project included the majority of front-end and back-end coding, using Python Flask, HTML, Firebase, Swift, and XCODE.

1NCUB8

SUMMER 2018 - PRESENT

1NCUB8 is a web app that allows teen start-ups to share their projects with potential investors and obtain the funding and expertise necessary to develop their ideas. It was initially created during the SF Kode with Klossy program. Responsibilities included the majority of front-end and back-end coding, using Python, Flask, and mongoDB. The app was submitted it to the Congressional App Challenge and won for the 15th District in California.

SanFrancisGO

SUMMER 2019 - PRESENT

SanFrancisGO is a mobile and web app initially created during the SF Kode with Klossy program in 2019. Responsibilities included front-end and back-end coding for the website, creating a database, and doing the majority of back-end work in Swift for the mobile app. For this project, used Swift, XCODE, CocoaPods, Python, Flask, and Firebase. The app was recently submitted it to the Congressional App Challenge.
