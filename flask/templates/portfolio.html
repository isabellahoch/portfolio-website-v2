PORTFOLIO

sendavirtualhug.com

APRIL 2020 - PRESENT

I wanted to create a resource to spread positivity during the pandemic, and I really miss hugging my friends! When my grandfather passed away in quarantine and I was unable to go visit and physically hug my grandmother or relatives, I decided to develop a web app so that I could send them a hug virtually! Thus, sendavirtualhug.com was born! For this project, I used a Python Flask backend with HTML/CSS/JS templates, Firebase, the Flask-Mail extension, and Twilio.

BUDDI

JANUARY 2020 - APRIL 2020

In light of the current gloabl pandemic, we wanted to create an app that would help one of the highest at-risk populations for COVID-19. During the 2020 PixelHacks IV Hackathon, we developed BUDDI, a companion tool that easily provides assistance and resources to senior citizens with a friendly chatbot and many other interactive features!This app ended up winning Best Use of Google Cloud and Domain at the hackathon. Responsibilities for this project included the majority of front-end and back-end coding, using Python Flask, HTML, Firebase, Swift, and XCODE.

1NCUB8

SUMMER 2018 - PRESENT

1NCUB8 is a web app that allows teen start-ups to share their projects with potential investors and obtain the funding and expertise necessary to develop their ideas. It was initially created during the SF Kode with Klossy program. Responsibilities included the majority of front-end and back-end coding, using Python Flask and mongoDB. The app was recently submitted it to the Congressional App Challenge.

SanFrancisGO

SUMMER 2019 - PRESENT

SanFrancisGO is a mobile and web app initially created during the SF Kode with Klossy program in 2019. Responsibilities included front-end and back-end coding for the website, creating a database, and doing the majority of back-end work in Swift for the mobile app. For this project, used Swift, XCODE, CocoaPods, Python, Flask, and Firebase. The app was recently submitted it to the Congressional App Challenge.
